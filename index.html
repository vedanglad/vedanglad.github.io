About me

Hey there! I am a cybersecurity graduate interested in offensive security, particularly vulnerability assessment and penetration testing. I enjoy breaking into systems (legally!), understanding how they work, and discovering ways to enhance their security.

This portfolio website showcases my career progress, including my work experience at various organizations, educational background from multiple institutions, skills, personal and academic projects, and published research.

I am always learning, experimenting, and seeking new challenges in the field of cybersecurity. To further develop my skills, I actively solve challenges on HackTheBox (HTB) and TryHackMe (THM), exploring different attack techniques and security concepts.

I also enjoy automating tasks whenever possible, whether writing helpful bash scripts to assist with my daily tasks or streamlining repetitive processes. This approach boosts efficiency and allows me to focus more on tackling real challenges.
